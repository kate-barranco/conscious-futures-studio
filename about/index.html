Why glasses? +

Our mark is a hand-drawn pair of glasses, designed by Abby Fish — a little wobbly at the edges, not a razor-clean vector arc. That's not an accident.

The future was never going to arrive polished and pre-rendered. It gets sketched, revised, and drawn again by the people paying the closest attention. Glasses are a lens, not a crystal ball — a tool for sharper sight into what's already emerging, not a promise of what's coming next.

Emergent, not polished. That's the whole idea.

"The future can't be predicted, but it can be envisioned and brought lovingly into being." — Donella Meadows
