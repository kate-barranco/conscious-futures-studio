What we do.

Conscious Futures is a research and strategy studio for civic and nonprofit organizations working through change that doesn't come with a clear playbook. Most engagements draw on four connected capabilities.

01 — Sensemaking

Research & Synthesis

Stakeholder interviews, landscape scans, and strategic insight development that surface what's actually happening, not just what's assumed.

02 — Facilitation & Strategy

Design & Facilitation

Workshop and futures-lab design, program and fellowship architecture, and convening that turns a room of people into real collective thinking.

03 — Narrative Change

Narrative & Knowledge

Narrative strategy, messaging architecture, and reports that carry an idea from insight into something people can act on.

04 — Futures & Foresight

Strategy & Foresight

Scenario planning, theory of change, and futures literacy work that helps an organization see past its next fiscal year.

The shape of an engagement follows the question — sometimes a focused sprint, sometimes an ongoing partnership.
